# PromptShield - Business Plan & Go-to-Market Strategy

**PromptShield** is a production-ready, enterprise-scale platform for detecting and mitigating prompt injection attacks on Large Language Model (LLM) applications. With 88.4% detection accuracy and <60ms latency, it addresses a critical security gap in the rapidly growing LLM market.

- **Total Addressable Market (TAM)**: $8.5B (LLM security market by 2027)
- **Projected 2027 Revenue**: $2M ARR
